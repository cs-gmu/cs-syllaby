SWE 619 Course Syllabus

Object-Oriented Software Specification and Construction

Spring 2009

Course Description

To give the students a solid understanding of modern software construction. To prepare students to construct sequential and concurrent programs. To encourage the construction of software systems of high quality. In-depth study of software construction in a modern language including control structuring and packaging. Concepts such as information hiding, data abstraction, and object-based and object-oriented software construction are discussed and illustrated.

Professor: James Baldo Jr.
