Overview
This course provides an introduction to the fundamental concepts in Deep Learning

  • Multilayer Perceptrons and algorithms to train them. Hidden units.
  • Backpropagation, Gradient descent and Stochastic Gradient Descent. .
  • Regularization for Deep Learning
  • Convolutional methods (CNNs).
  • Recurrent and recursive nets (RNNs).
  • Practical aspects and evaluation of Deep Learning
  • Autoencoders.
  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).

Prerequisites: CS 580, or CS 584, CS 688.

Textbook

  • The Science of Deep Learning by Iddo Drori. Cambridge University Press.
  • Dive into Deep Learning by Zhang et al (https://d2l.ai/)
  • Deep Learning with Python, 2nd edition by F. Chollet
